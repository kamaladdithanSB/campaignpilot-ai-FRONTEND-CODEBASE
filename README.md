### CAMPAIGN PILOT AI - FRONTEND CODEBASE

### AI Revenue Copilot for Modern Retailers

CampaignPilot AI is an AI-powered CRM intelligence platform that helps retailers transform raw customer data into revenue-generating marketing campaigns within minutes.

Instead of manually analyzing spreadsheets, identifying customer segments, creating campaign content, and estimating ROI, retailers simply upload their customer data and describe a business goal.

CampaignPilot AI handles the rest.

---

##  Key Features

### 📊 Smart Data Ingestion
- CSV Upload Support
- Shopify Integration
- Automatic Customer & Order Processing
- Real-time Data Activation

###  AI Revenue Intelligence
CampaignPilot AI automatically:

- Discovers customer segments
- Identifies revenue opportunities
- Detects churn risks
- Generates campaign strategies
- Estimates business impact

###  Intelligent Audience Selection

Examples:

- Win back inactive customers
- Increase average order value
- Boost repeat purchases
- Promote new product launches

The AI automatically selects the most relevant customer audience.

###  AI Campaign Generation

Automatically generates:

- Email campaigns
- SMS campaigns
- Audience reasoning
- Business impact analysis

###  Campaign Simulation Engine

Before launching a campaign, retailers can visualize:

- Audience behavior
- Conversion journey
- Revenue impact
- Campaign progression

through an interactive simulation experience.

###  ROI Analytics Dashboard

CampaignPilot AI visualizes:

- Revenue uplift
- Conversion improvement
- Customer engagement
- Before vs After campaign performance
